Frequently Asked Questions
What is United Wheelie Wash?
United Wheelie Wash is a professional wheelie bin cleaning service for residential and commercial customers. United Wheelie Wash has worked with a local fabricator to build a custom unit that carries and recycles its own water. Utilising high-pressure with biodegradable, environmentally safe solutions to clean, sanitise and deodorise your bins

How much does it cost?

Residential:

We offer three subscription plans with the intent for it to be as easy and stress free as possible to have clean healthy bins according to your usage. Gold, Silver or Bronze.

Gold subscription is the best value costing only $12 per bin with red, yellow and green bins cleaned once per month (This includes one free FOGO bin clean per cycle). This is billed every 6 months.

Silver subscription costs $15 per bin with red, yellow and green bins cleaned once per quarter (This includes one free FOGO bin clean per cleaning cycle). This is billed annually.

Bronze subscription costs depends on the number of bins you would like cleaned per month. This is billed monthly.

Service Cancellation Policies

We strive to offer flexible and transparent cancellation policies tailored to meet the needs of all our customers. Please review the details for each type of service below:

Gold Subscription

  • Details: $36 per month, billed every six months at $216, covering 12 services annually.
  • Cancellation: You may cancel anytime. The cancellation takes effect immediately upon confirmation.
  • Refunds: Eligible pro rata refunds are available after receiving six services. Refunds for unused months will be processed within 14 days.
  • Renewal: Automatically renews at the end of the annual billing cycle unless cancelled before the renewal date.

Silver Subscription

  • Details: $45 per quarter, billed annually at $180, covering four quarterly services.
  • Cancellation: You may cancel anytime. The cancellation takes effect immediately upon confirmation.
  • Refunds: Eligible pro rata refunds are available after receiving two services. Refunds for unused services will be processed within 14 days.
  • Renewal: Automatically renews at the end of the annual billing cycle unless cancelled before the renewal date.

Bronze Subscription

  • Details: Recurring monthly service priced between $15 and $18 per bin, depending on the number of bins required. A minimum subscription period of two months is required, and subscribers are obligated to fulfill this commitment before cancellation is permitted.
  • Cancellation: You may cancel only after completing the two-month minimum subscription period. The cancellation takes effect at the end of the current monthly billing cycle.
  • Renewal: Automatically renews monthly unless cancelled before the renewal date.

One-Off Services

  • Cancellation: Cancellations made up to 48 hours before the scheduled service time will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are not eligible for a refund.
  • Refund Processing: Refunds for eligible cancellations will be processed within 14 days.
  • Rescheduling: Rescheduling requests up to 48 hours before the scheduled service are penalty-free. Late rescheduling requests may incur additional fees.

Exceptions

  • Exceptional cases will be reviewed individually for resolution.
